Bacon Rosemary Stuffed Mushrooms

Description

These mushrooms are declectable and a sure crowd pleaser, especially with some delicious bacon and cheese!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablestoons butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • (8 ounce) package mushrooms, stems removed and chopped and caps reserved
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 and a quarter cups shredded Edam cheese
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, chopped

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat and cook onion until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add chopped mushroom stems and cook until soft, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon pieces on paper towels to add mushroom mixture. Stir in Edam cheese and rosemary and mix to combine. Fill mushroom caps with muxture and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is lightly browned and cheese is melted, 15 to 20 minutes.
