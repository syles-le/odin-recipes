Bacon Rosemary Stuffed Mushrooms

Description

These mushrooms are declectable and a sure crowd pleaser, especially with some delicious bacon and cheese!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablestoons butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • (8 ounce) package mushrooms, stems removed and chopped and caps reserved
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 and a quarter cups shredded Edam cheese
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, chopped
